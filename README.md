# Smart-Agri-Connect
The Integrated Assured Contract Farming and Post-Harvest Loss Reduction System connects farmers directly with buyers through digital contracts for assured prices and markets. It also provides storage, packaging, and transport guidance after harvest to reduce crop losses, improve farmer income, and support sustainable agriculture.
Farmers often face major challenges such as unstable crop prices, exploitation by middlemen, lack
of assured buyers, and significant post-harvest losses caused by improper storage, packaging, and
transportation. These issues reduce farmer income and contribute to large-scale food wastage. To

address these problems, this project proposes an Integrated Assured Contract Farming and Post-
Harvest Loss Reduction System that supports farmers throughout the complete agricultural cycle.

The system enables direct interaction between farmers and buyers through secure digital contracts
created before cultivation. In these contracts, both parties mutually agree on crop type, quantity,
quality standards, price, and delivery schedule, ensuring transparency and fair pricing. This
approach reduces market uncertainty, eliminates middlemen exploitation, and provides farmers
with guaranteed market access. After harvesting, the system assists farmers by collecting simple
inputs such as crop type, harvested quantity, and expected storage duration. Based on this data, it
offers practical recommendations for appropriate storage techniques, safe packaging methods, and
efficient transportation practices to minimize losses. By integrating assured contract farming with
post-harvest management guidance on a user friendly web platform, the proposed system aims to
reduce crop wastage, increase farmer profits, improve food quality, and promote sustainable
agricultural development, ultimately strengthening the
agriculture supply chain.
